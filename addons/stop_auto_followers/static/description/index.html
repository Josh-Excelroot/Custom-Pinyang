Stop Auto Followers

Key Features:

  • No Automatic Followers Add in Documents.
  • The app goal is to exclude automatic inclusion of users in followers. By default Odoo make all email followers. This may be dangerous in communication with external partners and may be annoying to your employees.
  • This module solves the issue but anyway leave you a tool to simply add followers.
  • By default partner are not followers.
  • A checkbox in an mail composer to force automatic followers.

A checkbox in an mail composer to force automatic followers.
